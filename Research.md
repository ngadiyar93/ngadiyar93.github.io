## Research
![image](/intelFreedom.gif)

My research is in the intersection of electric machine design, power electronics and control to realize power-dense, efficient, and cost-effective electric drive systems. My Ph.D. research focused on electrification of off-highway vehicles.

For a current list of my publications, please check out my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CPd0z5gAAAAJ&hl=en)

**Note 1:** My name appears as FNU Nishanth in most publications, as I only have one name. FNU stands for (First Name Unknown). You can read more about it [here](/Random.md). Some of my publications may have my name as Nishanth or . Nishanth.

**Note 2:** The machines I work on (atleast for now) have nothing (much) to do with the *Machine Learning* that people go crazy over. It is my usual (old) joke that I don't do _Machine Learning_ but I am "learning" _Machines_.
