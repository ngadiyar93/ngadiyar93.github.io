## Research
![image](/intelFreedom.gif)

My research is in the broad domain of Electric machine design, power electronics and control. Currently (as of 09/2020), I am working on design and optimization of an axial flux machine that will be integrated with a hydraulic pump to electrify off-highway vehicles.
My work is funded by the US department of energy. 

A list of selected publications is provided below. For a current list, please check out my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CPd0z5gAAAAJ&hl=en)

**Note 1:** My name appears as FNU Nishanth in most publications, as I only have one name. FNU stands for (First Name Unknown). You can read more about it [here](/Random.md)

**Note 2:** The machines I work on (atleast for now) have nothing (much) to do with the *Machine Learning* that people go crazy over. It is my usual (old) joke that I don't do _Machine Learning_ but I am "learning" _Machines_.
