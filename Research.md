## Research
![image](/intelFreedom.gif)

My research is at the intersection of electric machine design, power electronics and control, from milliwatt scale to the megawatt scale. I have a particular soft spot for multi-harmonic, topology optimized, and additively manufactured electric machines that unlock additional degrees of freedom.  

For a current list of my publications, please check out my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CPd0z5gAAAAJ&hl=en)

**Note 1:** My name appears as FNU Nishanth in most publications, as I only have one name. FNU (or its variants F.N.U. / Fnu) is short for (First Name Unknown). You can read more about it [here](/Random.md). Some of my publications may have my name as Nishanth or . Nishanth.

**Note 2:** The machines I work on (atleast for now) have nothing much to do with the *Machine Learning* that people go crazy over. I don't do intense _Machine Learning_ but I am "learning" _Machines_. That said, I am exploring the use of Machine Learning techniques to improve the design / performance of electric machines.
